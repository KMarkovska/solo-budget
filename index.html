Automatic saving

Your entries are saved in this browser on this device. They stay private, but they do not automatically appear in another browser or phone.

Move or back up your budget

Use Save budget file and keep that file in OneDrive or SharePoint. On another device, open the app and use Load budget file.

Readable spreadsheet

Use Open in Excel when you want a simple spreadsheet copy. The budget file is the one that keeps all app data.
